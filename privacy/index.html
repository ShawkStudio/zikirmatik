2. PHOTO AND VIDEO DATA & AI PROCESSING

This section applies to Apps that allow you to upload photos or videos for AI processing.

2.1 How We Process Your Photos and Videos

When you upload photos or videos to the App for AI transformation:

  • They are securely transmitted to our processing servers via encrypted connections.
  • They are processed by AI models operated by our third-party AI service providers (see Section 4.1) to generate the transformations you request.
  • Automatic Deletion: All uploaded photos and videos are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours of upload.
  • Generated content (Output Content) remains accessible to you until you delete it or terminate your account.
  • Usage Limits: Generations are subject to the fair-use limits described in Section 1.3.

2.2 Face Data & Image Processing

IMPORTANT: We do not collect, analyze, or store facial data or biometric identifiers.

All photos, including those that may contain faces, are processed solely to apply visual effects and AI-generated transformations (e.g., anime, cartoon, portrait, avatar, or fantasy styles).

  • No Facial Recognition: The App does not perform facial recognition, face mapping, or identity analysis.
  • No Biometric Data Collection: We do not create, infer, or retain any data that can identify or authenticate an individual based on facial features.
  • Artistic Rendering Only: The App's AI models process images exclusively for artistic rendering purposes and do not perform biometric analysis of any kind.
  • Temporary Processing: Photos are processed temporarily on secure servers and automatically deleted within 24 hours.
  • No Database Storage: We do not maintain any database of facial features, face templates, or biometric identifiers.
  • Third-Party Processing: Photos are processed by our third-party AI service providers (see Section 4.1), which operate under strict data processing agreements and do not retain your images after processing.

What We Do:

  • Apply artistic filters and style transfers to your photos
  • Generate creative transformations (anime, cartoon, fantasy, portraits, avatars, etc.)
  • Enhance images using AI algorithms
  • Process images to deliver the visual effects you request

What We DO NOT Do:

  • Identify individuals from facial features
  • Authenticate users based on face data
  • Create biometric profiles or templates
  • Match faces across different photos
  • Analyze demographic characteristics from faces
  • Store or retain any facial recognition data
  • Share face data with third parties for identification purposes

Purpose of Processing: The App uses uploaded photos containing faces strictly for generating the AI results you request. No facial recognition, face mapping, or identity analysis is performed.

2.3 Data Security During Processing

  • All photos and videos are transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL).
  • They are stored temporarily in secure, access-controlled storage during processing.
  • We implement strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access to uploaded content.
  • No Sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your uploaded photos or videos with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • No Model Training: Uploaded photos and videos are never used to train AI models or for any purpose beyond generating your requested output.

2A. KEYBOARD DATA & AI TEXT PROCESSING

This section applies to Apps that include a custom keyboard extension.

2A.1 No Keystroke Logging

IMPORTANT: Our keyboards do not log, record, collect, store, or transmit your keystrokes.

  • Regular typing — keys, word suggestions, emoji, haptics — happens entirely on your device and requires no network connection.
  • We do not maintain any record of what you type during normal typing, and nothing you type is sent anywhere automatically.

2A.2 When Text Is Transmitted

Text leaves your device only when you explicitly invoke an AI action (such as grammar correction, tone rewriting, translation, or Ask AI). At that moment:

  • The relevant text from the active text field is transmitted over an encrypted connection (TLS/SSL) to our third-party AI service provider (see Section 4.1), through a secure proxy layer.
  • The text is processed transiently, solely to generate the result you requested, and the result is returned to your keyboard.
  • We do not store the submitted text on our servers.
  • Under our providers' API data usage terms, content submitted via their APIs is not used to train their models.
  • Your text is never used for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes, and is never sold or shared with third parties beyond the processing described here.

2A.3 Full Access Permission

Apple requires custom keyboards to obtain the "Allow Full Access" permission before they can use the network. Our keyboards use Full Access solely to:

  • Send the text you explicitly submit to AI actions to our AI service provider and return the result;
  • Enable device features iOS restricts for keyboards without Full Access (such as haptic feedback);
  • Share your settings and subscription status between the main App and the keyboard, locally on your device.

Without Full Access, the keyboard continues to work for regular typing; only AI features and certain device features are unavailable.

2A.4 History (Stored on Your Device Only)

  • If the App offers a history feature and you enable it, AI results are stored locally on your device only, in the App's shared on-device storage.
  • History is never uploaded to our servers and is not accessible to us.
  • You can turn history off or clear it at any time from the keyboard or App settings, and it is removed when you delete the App.

2A.5 Secure Fields

iOS automatically disables custom keyboards in secure text fields (such as password fields). Our keyboards therefore cannot see or access anything you enter into such fields.
